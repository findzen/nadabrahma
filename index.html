About

A sonic mystic and singer/songwriter, Nada Brahma travels the world sharing practices of love through ceremony, kirtan, and workshops. Private sessions

One-on-one sound healing tailored to your needs

Each session is unique and tailored to the individual’s needs, typically beginning with a resonance diagnosis to locate areas of dissonance which may indicate energetic blockages or points of dis-ease in the system.

Vocal toning, chant, and various acoustic and electronic musical instruments are then employed using naturally occurring frequencies such as those found in bird songs, celestial movements, and ocean waves to bring the recipient’s system into coherence with its original state of wellbeing.

Sound baths

Ceremonial 432hz sound healing for groups of any size

Sound baths are extended meditations that use auditory frequencies as a catalyst for multi-dimensional healing and the achievement of deep states of consciousness.

Using a variety of acoustic and electronic instruments tuned to the sacred frequency of 432hz, recipients are guided through an experience that many have reported results in profound transformation, relaxation, rejuvenation, and altered states of awareness.
